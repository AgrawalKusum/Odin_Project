Fruit Cream

Thr Fruit cream is a deliciously nutritious dessert that will not only fulfil your daily vitamin intake but also satisfly our sugar cravings with its heavenly taste. And guess what! It is a fireless cooking recipe, no need to turn on the stove or heat up your oven...mix the ingredients and your are ready to go!

Ingredients

  1. Fresh Milk Cream
  2. Cardamoms
  3. Resins(Any and all the dryfruits you like)
  4. Apple(Any not so chewy fruit that does not having tons of seeds)
  5. Powdered Sugar

Steps

  1. Churn the cream to make its texture even and runny. Keep it cool to avoid churning it into butter
  2. Add grounded cardamoms and sugar, mix well and refrigerate
  3. Chop all the fruits and dry fruits to tiny cubes, or to the size and shape you like
  4. Add all the chopped fruits to the cream. Mix gently.
  5. Refregerate of at least 20 mins to allow fruit juices to mix with cream. Take out and stir.
  6. Serve chilled
